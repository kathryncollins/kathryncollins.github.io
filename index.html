I was born December 1, 1992 in Southern California. The first child of Timothy and Patricia Collins.

From 1995-2007, the family lived in a suburb of Houston, Texas. A second daughter, Colleen, and a puppy would join the family.

Kathryn displayed an aptitude for reading and storytelling at an early age, according to her -admittedly biased- mother.

In 2007, the Collins family decided to return to their roots in Boston. They currently reside there.

In high school, Kathryn discovered the school paper, tv station, debate, and drama club. She's been trying to figure out a way to make money from telling interesting stories since then.
